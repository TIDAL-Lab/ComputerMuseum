Frog Pond: Natural Selection

You control how the frogs in the pond behave by making programs
The only difference between frogs is their size...

A frog's size affects some of the things it does...

Hop:


Bigger frogs hop farther.
Wait for a fly:


Bigger frogs see farther.
Try to eat:


Bigger frogs have longer tongues.

Other things the frogs do have a bit of randomness...

Hatching:


Offspring grow up right away.
They may be a little bit bigger or smaller than their parent.
They will start a little bit away from their parent and facing in a slightly different direction.
Turning:


Frogs don't turn precisely.
"Right" tells a frog to turn a little to its right.
"Left" tells a frog to turn a little to its left
